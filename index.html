About Me

From a young age, I’ve been fascinated by how video games, websites, and technology are created. This curiosity sparked my desire to learn how to build and design them myself. In 2019, I enrolled in my school’s Early Middle College program, where I studied both high school and college-level courses in Computer Information Systems. This experience gave me insight into how the things I loved were made and inspired me to bring my own ideas to life.
